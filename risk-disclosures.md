## Additional risks relevant to BondX design

* **Incentive exploitation**: points systems can be gamed; mitigation must be continuously improved.
* **Reward sustainability**: "1 point = 1 BONDX" requires careful emission and redemption policy to avoid excessive supply pressure.
* **Admin-controlled operations**: certain operations (e.g., BondXCoin LP listing, rewards claiming enablement) may be admin-triggered depending on deployment configuration.
* **Multi-chain fragmentation**: liquidity and adoption can vary dramatically by chain, impacting token dynamics.
